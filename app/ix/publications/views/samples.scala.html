NCATS Publication and Project RSS Feed

The following pages make use of the NCATS Publication and Project RSS Feed. The feed is available at the following url: 

@{Global.getHost()+ix.publications.controllers.routes.ReachApp.rss("KEY")}
where KEY is the designated web-tag key generated by the Publication and Project curation tool. For example, to get the RSS feed for NCGC web-tag, use the this url 
@{Global.getHost()+ix.publications.controllers.routes.ReachApp.rss("NCGC")}
Note that permanent RSS feed urls for publications and projects are http://publications.ncats.nih.gov/rss and http://projects.ncats.nih.gov/rss, respectively. Right now the following are known web-tag's:
